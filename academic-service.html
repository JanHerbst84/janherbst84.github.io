Institutional Leadership

At the University of Huddersfield, I have held several significant leadership positions:

  • Research Coordinator for Music and Music Technology (UoA Lead) University of Huddersfield 2023 – present

    Leading the department's research strategy, conducting research audits, managing research budgets, and overseeing research environment development.

  • Director of the Centre for Music, Culture and Identity (CMCI) and Centre for Research in Music and its Technologies (CRMT) University of Huddersfield 2019 – 2025

    Overseeing budget and strategic investment, appointing visiting professors and fellows, and organizing research seminars and symposia.

  • Director of the Popular Music Studies Research Group (PMSRG) University of Huddersfield 2019 – 2020

    Weekly research tutorials with postgraduate researchers, plus selected meetings with colleagues and visiting scholars.

  • Head of Musicology University of Huddersfield 2017 – 2019

    Overseeing the musicological modules and coordination with relevant external examiners.
