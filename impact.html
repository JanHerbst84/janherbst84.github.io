Future Impact Directions

Looking forward, I am working to expand the impact of my research through several initiatives:

  • Open Science Integration: Increasing the accessibility and reproducibility of research through open methodologies and data sharing
  • Industry Partnerships: Developing formal partnerships with music technology companies and production studios to facilitate knowledge transfer
  • Educational Resources: Creating teaching materials and resources based on research findings for use in higher education and professional development
  • Public Engagement: Expanding media engagement to communicate research findings to wider audiences through accessible formats
  • International Collaboration: Building new research networks to address global challenges in music production and technology
