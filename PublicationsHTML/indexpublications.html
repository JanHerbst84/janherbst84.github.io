Books & Edited Volumes

This section includes authored and edited monographs, edited volumes, and special issues of journals. My books explore various aspects of popular music production, particularly focusing on the electric guitar and metal music production. Recent works include volumes published with Cambridge University Press and Routledge.

Notable publications include The Cambridge Companion to the Electric Guitar, The Cambridge Companion to Metal Music, and Gear Acquisition Syndrome: Consumption of Instruments and Technology in Popular Music.

Journal Articles

My journal articles cover a wide range of topics in popular music studies, music technology, and metal music research, published in leading peer-reviewed journals. Many articles analyse technical aspects of music production, the electric guitar's development, and cultural phenomena related to music technology.

These publications appear in journals including Metal Music Studies, Popular Music and Society, Rock Music Studies, Music Perception, and Current Musicology, among others.

Book Chapters & Conference Proceedings

This section contains contributions to edited volumes, conference proceedings, and handbook entries. These chapters often provide focused analyses of specific aspects of popular music, production techniques, or cultural phenomena related to music technology.
